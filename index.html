About Me

I am a self-taught web developer from Nairobi, kenya. I got into tech because i wanted to build tools that solve everyday problems --- the kind of problems i see around me in my community.

When I am not coding, you will find me exploring Karura Forest, trying new recipes for chapati variations, or reading about how tech companies in East Africa are solving local problems with global-quality software.

I am currently looking for opportunities in web development --- whether that is a junior developer role, a freelance project, or a collaboration on something meaningful.
